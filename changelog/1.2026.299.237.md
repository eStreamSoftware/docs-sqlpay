This release adds Tax CP8D for 2025, updates Borang EA & EC forms, and includes several fixes and improvements for the WebApp and payroll processing. (*237*)

<!-- truncate -->

## Features

- Added **Tax CP8D** for 2025
- Updated **Borang EA** & **EC** for 2025 & 2026

## Updates

- Moved **Maintain Loan** access right to **Loan** group
- Renamed grid column captions on **CP8D** data form

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed **WebApp** month-end payroll processing not including pending claim transactions (#6272)
- Fixed typo "Manger" on **WebApp** Maintain Employee entry form
- Fixed **Payroll Summary** data form missing **Advance Deduct** grid column (#6262)
- Fixed **Print PCB 2026** not populating "Identification No." (#6259)
- Fixed error "Interface not supported" when editing **EPF** on employee paysheet (#6258)
